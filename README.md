# AIPUSH

**Get recommended by ChatGPT, Perplexity & Gemini — automatically.**

AIPUSH is an AI-optimized page distribution platform that publishes structured, audit-compliant pages on your domain every day. When someone asks an AI assistant for a recommendation in your industry, AIPUSH makes sure your business is the answer.

**Live:** [https://aipush.app](https://aipush.app)

---

## What It Does

AIPUSH automates the entire process of getting your business recommended by AI assistants:

1. **AI Visibility Audit** — Free scan that shows how visible your business is to ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. Results in 10 seconds.

2. **Market Intelligence Profile (MIP)** — Automatically analyzes your business, competitors, keywords, and market gaps. Builds a complete competitive positioning strategy.

3. **Daily Page Generation** — Publishes 20 AI-optimized pages per day on your verified subdomain. Each page is compliance-verified, uniqueness-checked, and structured for AI citation.

4. **AI Visibility Tracking** — Monitors whether AI assistants recommend your business across multiple models and query types.

## Key Features

- **Fully automated** — no manual content writing, no SEO editing, no server configuration
- **Your domain** — pages published on `reference.yourdomain.com` with automatic SSL
- **20 pages/day** — continuous content generation with built-in audit gate
- **AI-first optimization** — structured HTML designed for LLM retrieval, not traditional SEO
- **Competitive intelligence** — 6-12 competitors analyzed, keyword gaps identified
- **Crypto payments** — USDT/USDC on Ethereum, 3-confirmation activation
- **MCP integration** — connect your own AI agent via [APIbase.pro](https://apibase.pro)

## How It Works

```
① Audit    → Free AI visibility scan (10 seconds)
② Profile  → MIP analyzes your market (5 minutes)
③ Generate → 20 pages/day, audit-verified
④ Publish  → Your domain, SSL, global CDN
```

First results in 24 hours. AI citations typically appear within 1-4 weeks.
